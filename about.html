Our Story

Fine jewelry made for those on a path of learning

The Beginning

Born from a Question

Jamyang Jewels grew from a simple question asked by our founder: what if the jewelry we wear could serve as more than ornament? What if it could be a teacher — quiet, present, always on the body?

Rooted in the tradition of Tibetan wisdom, the name Jamyang refers to the bodhisattva of wisdom — gentle melody, the sound of insight. It is a name we carry with reverence and an aspiration we build into every piece.

We are a small Singapore-based atelier, working slowly, intentionally, and with great love for our craft.

The Craft

Made by Hand, One at a Time

Every ring, pendant, bracelet and earring is handcrafted. We do not run production lines. We run one piece at a time, shaped by hand, finished slowly, and set with care.

We source both raw, uncut diamonds — wild in their crystalline form, carrying the earth's ancient geometry — and perfectly faceted cut diamonds, where precision reveals the full spectrum of light hidden within.

We also work with fancy colored stones: sapphires, emeralds, rubies, and rare finds — each chosen for the quality of its color, its depth, and its resonance with the piece's intention.

Our Philosophy

Jewelry as Practice

Our customers are people engaged in the work of inner transformation — studying wisdom traditions, cultivating compassion, learning to be present. They come to us not looking for status symbols, but for companions.

A ring worn on the hand becomes visible dozens of times each day. A pendant rests near the heart. These moments of noticing are invitations — to return, to remember, to practice.

We work with each customer to find or create a piece that holds meaning for them specifically. This is not fast fashion. This is slow jewelry — made to last a lifetime, and longer.

What We Believe

The Values We Work By

Slowness

We do not rush. Every piece takes as long as it needs. Quality is not negotiable, and neither is care.

Intention

Every piece is born with a specific meaning. We ask: what do you want this piece to remind you of? The answer shapes everything.

Authenticity

We work with genuine gemstones, ethically sourced. No synthetics. No shortcuts. What you receive is what we say it is.

Connection

We sell in person — at workshops and events. We believe fine jewelry deserves to be felt, held, and understood before it becomes yours.
