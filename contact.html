Common Questions

What People Ask Us

Do you sell online? ◆

Not currently. We believe fine jewelry should be experienced in person before it becomes yours. We sell at events, workshops, and by private appointment.

How do bespoke commissions work? ◆

We start with a conversation — via WhatsApp or in person — about what you're looking for and what meaning you'd like the piece to carry. We then propose a design, select the stone(s), and craft it by hand. Lead time is typically 4–8 weeks.

What is the price range? ◆

Each piece is priced individually based on the stone(s) and metalwork involved. Reach us via WhatsApp or at an event to discuss pricing for specific pieces.

Do I need to know anything about gemstones? ◆

Not at all. We guide every customer through the process with patience and care. We share what's relevant, when it's relevant. Our workshops are also a wonderful way to learn before choosing.
