What to Expect

Our Workshops

Before

Arrive & Settle

Each workshop begins with a moment of stillness — tea, quiet, and a brief introduction to the philosophy behind each piece. We slow down before we begin.

During

Explore & Choose

You'll be guided through the collection. We talk about the stones, the setting, the intention behind each piece. You try what calls to you. There is no pressure.

After

Take It Home

Each piece leaves with its intention card — a small written card with the meaning, the stone, and a simple practice to do while wearing it.
